Summer Sangria

Easy Traditional Sangria

Recipe by: Minimalist Baker

Ingredients

  • 1 (750 ml) bottle dry red wine
  • ½ cup orange liqueur (e.g., Cointreau)
  • 1 orange, thinly sliced
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup club soda or sparkling water

Directions

  1. In a large pitcher, combine the red wine, orange liqueur, and sugar.
  2. Add the orange and lemon slices. Stir gently to dissolve the sugar.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour (up to 4 hours) to let flavors meld.
  4. Just before serving, add the club soda and stir gently.
  5. Pour into glasses—include a few fruit slices in each—and enjoy!
